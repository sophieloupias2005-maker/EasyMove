I am an an american with EU citizenship

If you are working for a French company, your healthcare registration is usually handled through your employer via the "Régime Général."

Key Steps

  • Social Security Number: Your employer will register you with the CPAM (Caisse Primaire d'Assurance Maladie).
  • Carte Vitale: Once registered, you will receive a form to order your green "Carte Vitale" chip card.
  • Mutuelle: French law requires your employer to offer a private health insurance top-up (Mutuelle) and pay at least 50% of the cost.

Documents Needed

Typically, you will need to provide your employer with:

  • Your passport and visa/residence permit.
  • A birth certificate (with a French translation).
  • Your French bank account details (RIB) for reimbursements.
